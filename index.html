Learn About Bitcoin & Digitize Your Work To The World ...

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency without any single administrator which is sent from user to user via peer to peer Bitcoin network.

Verification of Transactions is done by network nodes through cryptography and these transactions are recorded on a distributed public ledger called Blockchain.

Although there are many other cryptocurrencies Bitcoin is most popular amongst all. Blockchain Technology was specifically developed for Bitcoin. Bitcoins are created as a reward for “MINING”.

When we say a Bitcoin it does not refer to some physical coin, but it actually is a software or to be precise a set of protocols and processes. Bitcoin was made by use of cryptography which is study of making and breaking codes

Advantages of Bitcoin Payment:

Some advantages of Bitcoin Payment are:

Private and Anonymous: The transactions in Bitcoin are completely Anonymous and Private. A person may can only know the address of wallet where it is sent and received. The owner of the address cannot be known

Payment Freedom: A person can transfer Bitcoin from anywhere to any part of the world without any intermediaries and without any payment limit.

Low or Minimum Fees: Bitcoin Transactions require low or very less fees.The fees has to be paid when one needs to process the transaction fast which is again very minimum.

You can Create your own Bitcoin: The mining which we already discussed is a process to generate new Bitcoins.

Disadvantages of Bitcoin Payment:

Degree of Acceptance::

Degree of Acceptance Large numbers of people are still unaware about Bitcoin.

Volatality: The increase or decrease in Bitcoin price is at a very high pace. Many investors due to this reason do not invest in Bitcoin.

Lack of Resources: Losing Bitcoin Wallet means we lose all the Bitcoins and cannot be regained unless we have Backed up the Wallet by using Backup Phrase Code.

Money Laundering and Black-market: Bitcoin was used by people operating in dark net in order to stay anonymous and not reveal their identity. In case of money laundering as well the middlemen collect the money from one source and transfer to other using Bitcoin Payment.
