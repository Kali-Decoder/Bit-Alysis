Some Important Topics Which Will Elaborate More About Bitcoin & Blockchain...

Key Elements of Blockchain:-

Ⓢ Distributed Ledger Technology: All the nodes on the blockchain network have access to the decentralized ledger and its immutable records. The transactions on the shared ledger are recorded only once.

Ⓢ Immutable Records: Any altering or tampering by any participant, in any transaction cannot be done once the transaction has been recorded. In case of an error in the recorded transaction, a new transaction is to be recorded in order to reverse this error, and both the transactions are visible..

Ⓢ Smart Contracts: In order to speed the transactions, a set of rules known as “SMART CONTRACTS” is stored and also executed automatically. They automatically record transaction on the ledger without any Human intervention. They eliminate all the paperwork, streamline the process and also save time and money. The conditions on these Smart Cards are mutually agreed upon by all participants on the network

Bitcoin Mining:-

Ⓢ Bitcoin Mining is a process of creating new Bitcoin by solving a computational puzzle. This process is required to maintain the ledger of Transaction .

Ⓢ Whenever someone sends Bitcoin to any part, it is termed as a “Transaction”. General day to day transactions are in form of physical receipt, or stored online or documented by banks.

Ⓢ The same thing is achieved by Bitcoin Miners by clumping the transactions together in the blocks which are added to the Blockchain which is a public record.The Nodes on the network maintain the records for any future verification

There are two Outcomes of this Mining:

1. New Bitcoins are produced by solving these complex maths problems

2. By doing this the miners are able to make the payment more trustworthy and secure by verifying the transaction details.

After successfully solving the puzzles the blocks are added to the Blockchain and the Bitcoin Miners are rewarded with few Bitcoins. Rest of the participants can sell or buy the tokens by

Crypto currency Exchange or peer to peer. The solving of these computational puzzles by Bitcoin Miners is the central idea of the Proof of Work (PoW) Consensus Algorithm. The node or the miner who solves this gets the privilege of mining the next block.
